## Overview

The WalletConnect Token (WCT) Season 1 airdrop identifies power users of the WalletConnect Network and invites them to become foundational members of our ecosystem. Through a sophisticated scoring system, we've identified users who deeply understand and actively engage with our network, offering them the opportunity to help shape the future of onchain UX through governance participation and staking. This distribution marks the beginning of WalletConnect's decentralized journey, where our most engaged users can have a direct say in the evolution of cross-chain interoperability standards.

## Airdrop Allocation

The Season 1 airdrop allocates a total of 50M WCT tokens distributed across three categories:

- **Community Distribution**: 30M WCT
  - Allocated to eligible users based on their network activity, on-chain presence, and airdrop behavior
 
- **Independent Contributors**: 10M WCT
  - Distributed to developers and contributors who have supported the ecosystem through GitHub contributions and Gitcoin donations

- **Strategic Partners**: 10M WCT
  - Reserved for partners who have played key roles in growing and supporting the WalletConnect ecosystem

<Note>
The scoring system detailed in this document determines the distribution of both the Community Distribution (30M WCT) and Independent Contributors (10M WCT) allocations, for a total of 40M WCT.
</Note>

## Distribution Process

The airdrop distribution follows a comprehensive evaluation process:

1. **Profile Creation and Validation**: Users create profiles through `airdrop.walletconnect.network`, connecting their wallets and optional GitHub accounts.

2. **Basic Eligibility Check**: Profiles are screened for basic requirements including WalletConnect Network usage.

3. **Scoring Evaluation**: Eligible profiles are evaluated across multiple dimensions using our scoring system.

4. **Additional Allocations**: Contributions through GitHub and Gitcoin are added to qualifying profiles.

5. **Final Screening**: A thorough risk assessment and Sybil resistance check is performed before finalizing the distribution list.

## Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for the Season 1 airdrop, users must have:
- Created a profile through `airdrop.walletconnect.network`
- Authenticated with at least one wallet using the WalletConnect Network
- Demonstrated activity through at least one connection or signature through the WalletConnect Netwrok before September 12, 2024
- Not have any addresses linked to their profile that appear in the OFAC SDN list.

<Note>
These are minimum requirements. Meeting them does not guarantee eligibility for the airdrop, as final distribution is determined by the comprehensive scoring system detailed below.
</Note>

## Scoring System

The airdrop scoring mechanism evaluates participants across three main pillars:

### 1. Network Activity
- Measures user interactions within the WalletConnect Network
- Evaluates connections made and signatures performed
- Uses a network effect multiplier based on user's activity ratio compared to the total network activity

### 2. On-Chain Presence
Activity across ten major blockchain networks is evaluated from June 12, 2024, to September 12, 2024, including:
- Ethereum
- BNB Smart Chain
- Polygon
- Avalanche
- Arbitrum
- Base
- Linea
- Optimism
- zkSync Era
- Blast

Key metrics include:
- Wallet balances (for a full list of tokens, see the Token List section [below](#token-list))
- Transaction volumes over three months
- Accumulated gas fees
- NFT minting activity

### 3. Airdrop Behavior
The system includes a reward/penalty mechanism based on how users handled previous airdrops:
- Long-term holders can receive up to a 5% bonus
- Quick sellers may face up to an 80% reduction
- Both retention rates and holding duration are considered

The airdrops evaluated include:
- ZK (zksync)
- vCow(Ethereum Mainnet)
- SAFE(Ethereum Mainnet)
- BLAST(Blast)
- BLUR(Ethereum Mainnet)
- MEME(Ethereum Mainnet)
- UNI(Ethereum Mainnet)
- LXP(Linea)
- ARB(Arbitrum)
- 1INCH(Ethereum Mainnet)
- DYDX(Ethereum Mainnet)

## Additional Allocations

The distribution model includes additional allocations that stack with the base scoring:
- **GitHub Contributions**: Recognition for technical contributions to the ecosystem
- **Gitcoin Donations**: Acknowledgment of community support through alternative channels

<Note>
These additional allocations are added to the base scoring, providing comprehensive recognition for ecosystem participation across different channels.
</Note>

## Protection Mechanisms

To maintain ecosystem integrity, several protection mechanisms are in place:

- **Bot Detection**: Sophisticated heuristic system analyzing transaction patterns to identify automated behavior.
- **Sybil Resistance**: Partnership with OpenBlocksLab for detecting duplicate or fraudulent profiles.
- **Risk Assessment**: Comprehensive screening protocols for compliance and security.
- **Sanctions Screening**: Automatic exclusion of addresses listed in the OFAC SDN list.
